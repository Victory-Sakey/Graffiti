Who Am I?

Greetings, I'm Precious Okon, also known as Graffiti. Currently, I'm pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering at the University of Uyo in Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. You might be wondering, "Why would an artist study Computer Engineering?" Well, let me share that I deliberately chose this path because I am both an artist and a tech enthusiast. The fascinating intersection of art and technology has always captivated me, and I've been working on merging these two passions to create something meaningful. I opted for computer engineering to gain technical proficiency while leveraging my existing artistic skills. If you're interested in joining me on this journey of bringing art to life, I invite you to become part of our community. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or an artist, your collaboration is welcome. Together, let's combine our skills and creativity to achieve the seemingly impossible. For those curious about the synergy between tech and art, or if you want a visual representation of what I'm describing, click the "Watch Video" button below. Additionally, you can view my artworks in the gallery section. Explore my designs; you might find something that resonates with you. I also provide art services such as wall art painting, and I assure you that my designs will leave a lasting impression.
